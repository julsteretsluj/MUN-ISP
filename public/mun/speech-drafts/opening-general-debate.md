# Opening General Debate — Draft (90s)

Chair, esteemed delegates,

Our delegation rises at a hinge moment: crises no longer queue politely. We see **climate shocks**, **forced displacement**, and **digital fragmentation** reinforcing one another — not sequentially, but simultaneously.

This committee must convert diagnosis into **operational resilience**: financing that arrives *before* famine curves, education systems that survive disruption, and accountability that travels with the vulnerable — not only with headlines.

We stand ready to build coalitions that pair ambition with implementation.

Thank you.
