# Research Brief — Water Security & South Asia

## Thesis
India’s groundwater stress intersects climate volatility with agricultural demand. Delegates should foreground **Integrated Water Resources Management (IWRM)** and bilateral data-sharing frameworks.

## Key treaties & bodies
- **Indus Waters Treaty (1960)** — Permanent Indus Commission
- **RAMSAR**, **UN-Water** — norms for sustainable use

## Negotiation anchors
1. Transparency on hydrological telemetry (seasonal basin models)
2. Financing for drip irrigation pilots in stressed districts
3. Disaster coordination during monsoon anomalies
